Space Blade

Project Info

Prototype idea of a space shooter style android game. The ship uses a revolving blade which can be rotated and thrown to attack enemies. If enemies crash into the revovling blade at at the correct angle, the enemies will be destroyed. This project was more of a self-learning project used to teach myself the basics of unity. This project was not completed due to circumstances, but it may be continued/re-created in the future since the idea has potential.
